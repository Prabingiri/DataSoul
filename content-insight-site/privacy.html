Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DataSoul Studio (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles the limited information you provide when you visit our website or use our scheduling services (the “Services”).

2. Information We Collect

  • User‑provided data: captions, media URLs, and platform credentials you enter explicitly.
  • Operational logs: IP address and user‑agent for security & troubleshooting (auto‑deleted after 30 days).

3. How We Use Information

  • Schedule and publish posts you authorize.
  • Maintain security and reliability of the Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing

We never sell your data. We share only:

  • With social platforms solely to publish the posts you request.
  • With infrastructure providers (e.g., Vercel, Supabase) under confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law.

5. Security

We use industry‑standard safeguards—HTTPS, encryption‑at‑rest, and least‑privilege controls—to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

Credentials and scheduling data are retained only as long as needed to perform the Services and deleted upon request.

7. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Choices

  • Email privacy@datasoul.studio to request data deletion.
  • Revoke social‑media tokens directly in those platforms at any time.

9. Updates

We may update this Policy; material changes are posted here with a new date.

10. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@datasoul.studio.
