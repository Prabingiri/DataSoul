Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DataSoul Studio (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles the limited information you provide when you visit our website or use any of our scheduling services (the “Services”).

2. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide to schedule posts: captions, media URLs, and platform-specific credentials you enter explicitly.
  • Essential operational logs: IP address and user-agent for security/troubleshooting, auto-deleted within 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule and publish posts to the social-media accounts you authorize.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only:

  • With social platforms solely to publish the posts you request.
  • With hosting providers (e.g., Vercel, Supabase) under confidentiality to operate the Service.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards—HTTPS, encryption-at-rest, least-privilege controls—to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

Credentials and scheduling data are kept only as long as necessary to perform the Services you request and deleted on request.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@datasoul.studio.
  • You may revoke social-media tokens directly in those platforms at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Material changes are posted here with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

Questions? Email privacy@datasoul.studio.
